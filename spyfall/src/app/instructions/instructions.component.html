Spyfall is a game of intrigue, deception and silly questions! It's a lot of fun for groups of 3-7 people!

You are all on the same team, except for one person who is chosen to be a spy. You only know who you are! You have no idea about anyone else.

The team shares the same location with different roles. The spy doesn't know the location, and it's their job to learn the location!

The most suspicious looking player goes first! He should ask any other player a question. There's no restriction what it can be. Whoever has been asked will be the next asker, and he/she can choose to question anyone he/she wants. Repeat this process to determine which of your teamates is the spy, or try to guess the location if you're a spy.

When the time runs out, the spy should reveal themself, and take a guess at the location. If he's correct, he wins. Else, he loses immediately. After that the team will vote for who they think is a spy. If the majority of them vote for the wrong person, the spy wins. Otherwise the team wins.

The voting/guessing can also be done during the middle of the game.
